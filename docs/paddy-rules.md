# Paddy Power Bet Builder — settlement rules that affect us

Researched 12 Jun 2026 from official help-centre pages (some via search excerpts —
PP blocks automated fetches) and the Super Sub promo T&Cs. Items marked ⚠ unverified.

## Player legs: void vs lose

- **Player never plays** (unused sub / not in squad): leg is **VOID and the builder
  is repriced** on remaining legs — not lost. If one leg remains, it stands as a single.
- **Player comes on as a sub**: leg is **LIVE** regardless of minutes — it settles on
  actual output and can lose. (First goalscorer only: void if the first goal came
  before they entered.)
- **Implication for placement timing**: placing before lineups is less dangerous than
  it looks for goalscorer/shots legs — a benched player voids the leg (price shrinks,
  bet survives) rather than killing it. The real lineup risk is a player starting on
  the bench and getting 20 low-value minutes.

## Super Sub (exact mechanics)

- Triggers **only when the player is substituted OFF** — never for non-starters.
- Bet rolls to the player who replaces them on the pitch (Opta-determined), and
  chains through subsequent substitutions, 90 minutes only.
- Covers player-outcome markets broadly (goals, assists, shots, SOT, fouls, tackles,
  cards, 1+/2+/3+ lines). On 2+/3+ lines the original player's stats ACCUMULATE
  with the sub's.
- **Cash out is removed permanently once a substitution affects the bet.**
- Valid on World Cup matches (promo runs to July 2027).

## Settlement gotchas (Opta definitions)

- **Shots on target**: woodwork does NOT count (unless the market is explicitly
  "incl woodwork"); a shot blocked by any defender other than a last-line block
  does NOT count; keeper-caught crosses don't count.
- **Own goals never count** for any goalscorer market; settlement uses the
  on-the-day goal attribution (later panel changes ignored).
- **Corners**: settled on corners TAKEN, not awarded.
- **Assists**: none for own goals, direct FKs, direct corners, or penalties won.

## Bet lifecycle rules

- **Postponed match** (not rescheduled within 3 days): affected legs void,
  builder repriced.
- **Abandoned**: legs already unconditionally determined stand; the rest void,
  builder repriced. ⚠ exact single-match-builder wording unverified.
- **Cash out**: available on builders but suspendable at any time; embeds an
  unpublished extra margin (industry consensus ~5–10%, ⚠ unverifiable) — the
  research consensus is cash-out is structurally -EV except against stale prices.

## Promos in force for WC 2026 (⚠ verify current T&Cs in-app before relying)

- **Bet Builder Insurance: money back as a FREE BET (up to £10) if exactly one
  leg of a 4+ leg builder loses.** Directly relevant: every bet we place is a
  4-leg builder, and sole-killer losses are our biggest loss category. CHECK
  OPT-IN STATUS before placing.
- "Golden Boot": free bet builder per shot on target by a chosen player.
- Super Sub (above). "Win More" badge: ⚠ meaning undocumented anywhere —
  likely the Super Sub stat-accumulation indicator; confirm via support chat.
